## What does an AI-search consultant actually do?

Most people hire generalists. Generalists optimize for Google’s blue links. That doesn’t help when a user asks ChatGPT for recommendations.

An AI-search specialist audits the model’s knowledge graph. They look at how the AI constructs its answer about your niche. Then they intervene.

The work has three parts:

1.  **Entity Resolution:** We tell the AI who you are. We fix structured data on your site so the model doesn’t guess your industry or location.
2.  **Signal Building:** We create off-site content that the AI trusts. This isn’t just backlinks. It’s authoritative mentions in places the model reads to form its "facts."
3.  **Continuous Testing:** We run daily queries. If the AI changes its mind, we adjust the site signals immediately.

It is manual. It is tedious. It is necessary.

## How is this different from an SEO agency?

SEO agencies focus on ranking for keywords. They want you to click their link.

AI-search consultants focus on being the source. They want your name in the AI’s answer text.
